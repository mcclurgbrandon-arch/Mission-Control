# Physics — Unit 9: Simple Harmonic Motion (SHM)

## Overview
Two-week unit covering simple harmonic motion, mass-spring systems, energy in SHM, graphs, and the simple pendulum. Includes formative micro-quiz and unit test.

## Day-by-day plan

**Day 1 (Tue Apr 7)**
*Intro to SHM & Uniform Circular Motion (7.1)*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Quick demo/animation linking circular motion to SHM; slides 1–10 and WS9.1 guided notes (in-class paired work).

**Homework:**
- SBP 6–10.

**Day 2 (Wed Apr 8)**
*Period & Frequency (7.2)*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Short Edpuzzle clip on frequency vs period; practice problems and rapid share-out; slide examples to calculate period/frequency.

**Homework:**
- None.

**Day 3 (Thu Apr 9)**
*Mass-Spring Intro (7.3)*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Mini-lesson on Hooke's law and mass-spring setup; slides 30–47 and start WS9.2 in class with scaffolded examples.

**Homework:**
- HW9.2 (first 2 problems).

**Day 4 (Fri Apr 10)**
*Mass-Spring Motion (7.3)*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Focused walkthrough of displacement/velocity/acceleration relations and key positions (A, 0, -A); complete WS9.2 in groups.

**Homework:**
- Finish HW9.2.

**Day 5 (Mon Apr 13)**
*Micro-Quiz (Mass-Spring)*
**Instructional Activities:**
- 10–15 minute micro-quiz; debrief and short intro to energy in SHM using one worked example.

**Homework:**
- Energy practice problems.

**Day 6 (Tue Apr 14)**
*Energy in SHM (7.4)*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Brief demo and slide derivation of kinetic and potential energy exchange; practice energy calculations with partner problems.

**Homework:**
- Energy problems.

**Day 7 (Wed Apr 15)**
*SHM Graphs (7.3)*

**Homework:**
- None.

**Homework:**

**Day 10 (Mon Apr 20)**
*Unit Test (SHM)*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Unit test: MC + FR covering mass-spring, energy, graphs, pendulum, conceptual reasoning.
