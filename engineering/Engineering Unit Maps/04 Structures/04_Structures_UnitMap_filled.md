# Unit 4 - Structures — Activities/Methods/Strategies (Days 1–12)

**Day 1 (Tuesday)**
*Internal Combustion*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Review simple shapes and centroid concept using guided notes.
- Instructor-led demonstration using Slides (Unit 4 - Centroids.pptx, slides 1–10).
- Guided practice: HW4.1 Centroid of Basic Shapes (paired work).

**Assessments:**
- Formative: observation during guided practice; check completion of HW4.1.

**Day 2 (Wednesday)**
*Centroids: Complex Shapes*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Lab/demo: sample problems introducing centroids of complex shapes.
- Group work: begin HW4.2 - Centroids of Complex Shapes; teacher circulates.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: in-class completion and correctness checks for HW4.2.

**Day 3 (Thursday)**
*Centroid Methods*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Mini-lesson: multi-shape centroid decomposition and common pitfalls.
- Structured practice: continue HW4.2 and work through Centroids_Guided_Notes exercises.
- Differentiation: provide worked examples and extension problems.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: guided-notes completion; spot-check problems.

**Day 4 (Friday)**
*Compound Systems Application*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Team design challenge: compute centroids of composite objects (HW4.3).
- Use 2.1.2.A IntroductionStructuralMemberProperties.pptx for structural context.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: team presentations and rubric-scored design solutions.

**Day 5 (Monday)**
*Consolidation*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Review session on HW4.3 problems and targeted remediation.
- Practice test problems from guided notes and past quizzes.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: practice assessment to identify students needing support.

**Day 6 (Tuesday) — Quiz Day**
*Centroid Assessment*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Quiz logistics and administration.
- Students complete Centroid Quiz1 under test conditions.

**Assessments:**
- Summative: Centroid Quiz1 (collect and grade; key in confidential folder).

**Day 7 (Tuesday)**
*Moments of Inertia — Simple Shapes*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Recap centroid concepts and relation to area distribution.
- Introduce Moments of Inertia for simple shapes with derivations.
- Guided practice: HW4.4 — Moments of Inertia of simple shapes (rectangles, circles, axis shifts).
- Notes page: WS4.4 Moment of Inertia Guided Notes

**Assessments:**
- Formative: check HW4.4 completion and accuracy.

**Day 8 (Wednesday)**
*Moments of Inertia — Complex Shapes & Parallel-Axis*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Teach decomposition of complex shapes and use of composite methods.
- Apply the parallel-axis theorem to complex shapes with worked examples (use WS4.4).
- Group practice: HW4.5 — Moments of Inertia for complex shapes.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: review HW4.5; address calculation errors and conceptual misunderstandings.

**Day 9 (Thursday)**
*MOI Lab & Measurement*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Hands-on lab: measure/compute MOI for fabricated shapes in teams.
- Instructor-led analysis comparing measured vs. calculated values; discuss sources of error.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: lab write-ups and rubric feedback; verify HW4.5 calculations.

**Day 10 (Friday)**
*Advanced MOI Methods*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Introduce composite shapes with holes and tabulated MOI values.
- Independent practice: HW4.6 — MOI for more complex shapes assigned.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: evaluate HW4.6 and provide targeted feedback.

**Day 11 (Monday)**
*Review & Synthesis*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Comprehensive review across simple → complex MOI problems.
- Practice test to prepare for summative assessment.

**Assessments:**
- Formative: practice assessment; identify students needing remediation.

**Day 12 (Tuesday) — MOI Quiz**
*Moments of Inertia Assessment*
**Instructional Activities:**
- Final brief review and clarify last-minute questions.
- Students complete MOI quiz under test conditions.

**Assessments:**
- Summative: Moments of Inertia Quiz (file to be prepared).
